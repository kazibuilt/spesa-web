FAQ

  • What is Paycheck Advance?

    Paycheck Advance allows you to access a portion of your next paycheck before it hits your account. With Paycheck Advance, you can spot yourself with up to $500 so you're covered when it matters most. Now, there's no need to worry about running out of cash between paydays.

  • Will using Paycheck Advance impact my credit score?

    Using Paycheck Advance will have no impact on your credit score. You can think of Paycheck Advance as a way to access your funds when you need it most.

  • How often can I take a Paycheck Advance?

    You can take a Paycheck Advance as many times as you'd like up to the amount that you qualify for.

  • How does repayment work?

    Repaying your Paycheck Advance is simple and easy! When your next payroll deposit arrives in your Current account or connected deposit account, the funds will be collected automatically from that deposit to repay your outstanding Paycheck Advance. Alternatively, you can choose to repay your outstanding Paycheck Advance early by visiting the Paycheck Advance screen and tapping on “Repay Advance.”

  • How do I become eligible for Paycheck Advance? Is there a way to check in the app?

    To qualify for Paycheck Advance, you must receive Eligible Payroll Deposits into your Current Account or to an external bank account that you connect through Current. There are also other qualifying criteria that may apply. You can check if you’re currently eligible for an advance in the Current app by going to the “Services” tab and selecting “Paycheck Advance.”

  • Where do I go to learn more about Paycheck Advance?

    Chat with us live in the app. We're available 24/7 to answer your questions. Or visit our FAQ.
